## ‚ö° Two Ways to Use

ZeroPrompt is flexible. Use it as your daily AI assistant or as a developer tool.

### 1. Chat Mode ("ChatGPT" as a Service)
*Best for: Daily use, research, and coding assistance.*

Stop paying monthly fees for subscriptions you don't use 24/7.
- **How it works:** You **pre-fund** your account (Vault) with **AVAX**.
- **The Experience:** Connect your wallet, deposit $5 worth of AVAX, and start chatting.
- **The Benefit:** The cost of each message is deducted from your balance in real-time. Switch from GPT-4 to Mistral to Flux Pro in the same chat window instantly.

### 2. Developer Mode (The x402 Protocol)
*Best for: Scripts, Apps, and AI Agents.*

This is the "atomic" way to pay. No deposits required‚Äîjust pay per request.
- **How it works:** Your code sends a request, receives a price quote (HTTP 402), and signs a payment authorization instantly.
- **Payment Options:**
  - **USDC:** Gasless payments via EIP-3009 (Server sponsors the gas).
  - **AVAX:** Standard direct payments.
- **The Flow:** `Request ‚Üí 402 Error (Price) ‚Üí Sign & Pay ‚Üí Get Response`.
